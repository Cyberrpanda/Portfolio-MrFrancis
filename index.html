Metering Application Automation

Mar 2025 - Nov 2025
Eko Electricity Distribution Plc
Completed

Project Overview

The metering process was originally manual, paper-based, and time-consuming, resulting in delays, incomplete data, customer complaints, and tracking inefficiencies. This project automated all workflows, enabling customer self-service, digital presurvey capture, installation confirmation, QAQC inspection, & meter programming all within one platform built by the Dev. team.

Solution covered 3 core phases:
  • Pre-Installation: Customer application, presurvey scheduling, survey data capture
  • Meter Installation: Vendor allocation, onsite installation reporting, meter number capture
  • QAQC & Programming: Quality validation, rejection/correction workflows, meter-to-account mapping, vending activation

My Contributions

  • Facilitated end-to-end requirement gathering, stakeholder workshops, and process definition
  • Produced SRDs, BRDs, workflow diagrams, user stories & UAT scripts
  • Engineered the full process flow for application, presurvey, installation, QAQC, and programming
  • Coordinated DevOps, Vendors, QAQC, & field engineers to ensure requirement alignment

Business Impact

  • Eliminated manual bottlenecks and reduced turnaround times across all districts
  • Improved customer satisfaction through better transparency and faster processing
  • Strengthened revenue assurance with accurate meter-account mapping

Summary

I led the BA and implementation of EKEDC's Metering App Project, transforming the meter deployment process into a modern, efficient, and compliant digital workflow that enabled seamless activation for thousands of customers.
